What to Expect

DevFestMN, established as one of the best developer conferences in the Midwest, will have a single day event focusing on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

DevFestMN, since 2013, has grown to a well known event, drawing people from around the world to previous events. In the past we have had nearly 600 attendees with 50+ speakers.

This year's 2024 DevFestMN Focus event will be a single track event covering Google platforms including Web, Android, AI, and Cloud.
